    üí∞ Inquiry ‚Üí Fees ‚Üí Payment Flow

    This flow is used for most transactional services in our ecosystem. It enables clients to:

    • Retrieve billing data
    • Calculate exact fees
    • Execute and finalize the payment

    üîÅ Flow Diagram

    graph LR
    A[Inquiry] --> B[Fees]
    B --> C[Payment]
    C --> D{Optional Steps}
    D --> E[Retry]
    D --> F[Confirm]
    D --> G[Cancel]
    D --> H[Check Status]

    • Bill Payments

    • Vouchers

    • Installments

    • Booking

    • Donations
